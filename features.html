Features

From Signal to Action — The AI Incident Assistant That Thinks With Your Team

Unified Data Ingestion

A complete picture, built automatically

The assistant continuously ingests and correlates data across your operational ecosystem — bulk structured and unstructured — to build a living understanding of your systems and teams.

Capabilities include:

  • Alerts: PagerDuty, OpsGenie, Datadog
  • Tickets: Jira, GitHub Issues, ServiceNow
  • Communications: Slack threads, Zoom transcripts
  • Code & Configs: GitHub, Bitbucket, Terraform state

Outcome: Your incident response starts with context — not guesswork.

Visualization: Unified Data Ingestion

Incident Observation & Timeline Building

Every action captured. Every decision understood.

Once an incident begins, the assistant shifts into observation mode — quietly and continuously building an accurate record of what unfolds.

Capabilities include:

  • Actions taken by engineers across tools
  • Decision signals: Slack annotations, log pattern changes
  • Key milestones: acknowledged, mitigated, resolved

Outcome: Incidents are documented as they happen — not reconstructed later.

Visualization: Incident Observation & Timeline Building

Real-Time AI Recommendations

The right insight, at the right moment.

Powered by AI and a continuously evolving knowledge graph, the assistant actively supports responders during an incident — without interrupting their flow.

Capabilities include:

  • Pattern Matching: Detects similar past incidents
  • Dynamic Suggestions: Surfaces relevant logs, commands, runbooks
  • Next-Step Nudges: Suggests the most likely diagnostic step

Outcome: Faster resolution, fewer dead ends, and better decisions under pressure.

Visualization: Real-Time AI Recommendations

Automated Documentation & Organizational Memory

Turn every incident into a lasting asset.

Once an incident is resolved, the assistant automatically transforms the response into structured, reusable knowledge — without adding work for your team.

Capabilities include:

  • Postmortems: Complete timelines, decisions, root causes
  • Runbooks: New learnings added, outdated steps deprecated
  • Onboarding Cards: Clear summaries for new engineers

Outcome: Every incident strengthens your organization — not just your uptime.

Visualization: Automated Documentation & Organizational Memory

The Result

Incidents handled better. Knowledge that compounds. Teams that scale.

From first alert to long-term learning, the assistant ensures nothing is lost — and everything gets smarter over time.
