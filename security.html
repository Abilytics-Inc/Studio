Security

Protecting your data, ensuring trust.

At Abilytics Inc, security is a top priority. Our AI assistant works with sensitive incident, collaboration, and code data across multiple services, and we take every measure to keep it safe.

Data Protection

Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest.

Access Controls

Only authorized team members and systems can access your data.

Monitoring

Continuous monitoring for unusual activity or potential threats.

Data Isolation

Your data is logically separated from other customers.

Incident Data Security

Secure Processing

Actions, logs, tickets, alerts, and conversations are handled securely.

Protected Insights

Metadata and AI-generated insights are protected to prevent unauthorized exposure.

Secure Integrations

Trusted Connections

Our platform integrates with tools like Slack, Jira, GitHub, ServiceNow, AWS, Azure, and monitoring platforms.

Best Practices

We use secure APIs and follow industry best practices to maintain data integrity across all integrations.

AI and Knowledge Graph Security

Secure Environment

All recommendations and knowledge graph updates occur within secure, controlled environments.

Protected Storage

Historical incident data and runbooks are stored safely and linked only to authorized users.

Compliance & Best Practices

Industry Standards

We follow industry-standard security frameworks and continuously review our policies.

Regular Audits

Regular audits, access reviews, and vulnerability testing are conducted to maintain high security standards.

Your Role in Security

Protect Credentials

Protect your account credentials and manage connected integrations carefully.

Manage Access

Only grant access to trusted team members and review permissions regularly.

Report Issues

Report any suspicious activity immediately to security@abilytics.com

Our Commitment to Security

Security is not a feature — it's the foundation of everything we build. We're committed to protecting your data and earning your trust every day.

Have Security Questions?

Our security team is here to answer your questions and address any concerns.
